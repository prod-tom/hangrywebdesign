"The Remedy for Last Nights Regrets"

About Our Restaurant

Hangry is a unique restaurant chain designed to satisfy the cravings we all have after an unforgettable night out. Rather than focusing on a single type of cuisine, Hangry brings together a curated menu of the ultimate comfort foods that people crave when they're feeling ravenous and maybe a little worse for wear. From the typical chicken fillet roll to a scrumptious spice bag, Hangry is here to turn those post-party cravings into a satisfying, flavorful experience and simultaneous overindulgence cure.
